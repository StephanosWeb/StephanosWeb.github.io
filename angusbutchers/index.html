Our Fresh Meat Display

At Angus Butchers you will find fresh meat daily, non-frozen and a selection of fresh sausages and home made 6oz steak burgers.

British Quality

We have a vast amount of selective choice of steaks including british steak fillets and put scottish sirloin steak alone with all your diced meats and lamb and pork joints.

Our Variety

Our display area will offer you a variety of continental loin smoked meats including Greek and Polish sausages, pitta breads, Greek halloumi cheese and lots more.

Angus Butchers are purveyors of the kind of meat you'll always want on your plate. Being perfectionists, we choose the finest, naturally reared meat from native, traditional and rare breeds and dry-age on the bone. The result is a melting succulence and deep, disting flavour. All our meat is cut and wrapped to order at our premisses. So, whether it's a feasting centerpiece or some lesser-known offal, we're always happy to deal with our customer requests.

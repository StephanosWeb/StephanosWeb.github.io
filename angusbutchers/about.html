About us

Angus Butchers is based on the belief that our customers needs are of the utmost importance. Angus Butchers team is committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referalls. Angus Butchers is a family run business and offer a wide range of freshly selective meats at affordable prices, meaning not only we offer quality but out aim is always to offer competive prices compared to the four main supermarkets. Angus Butchers also specialise in game. We have pleasure in offering our homemade Angus burgers and cumberland sausages using natural sausage skins, flavours vary due to seasonal produce.
